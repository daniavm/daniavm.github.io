Principia × Villa María Academy

Modernizing Data Management for Academic Leadership

An alliance to transform data into a pillar of pedagogical accompaniment, strengthen school climate and preserve pedagogical knowledge — laying the foundations for an Innovation Area.

Our Implementation Journey

Phase I

Phase II

Solve & Demonstrate

Phase III

Formalize & Grow

Specific Objectives

This document details a 5-month initial collaboration to set operational and technological foundations and co-design tools that justify creating an Innovation Area in 2026.

Expected Results

A strategic proposal for an Innovation Area.
A data architecture with automated flows in progress.
A functional prototype of the Comprehensive Student Profile.
A clear roadmap for Innovation (2026-2027).

Work Plan: 5 Strategic Phases

A condensed timeline to deliver prototypes, stabilize flows and build the case for an Innovation Area.

Phase 1 — Strategic Proposal & Foundations

Deliver a validated proposal for the Innovation Area and stabilize technical access and core automations.

Duration: Month 1

Phase 2 — Co-design Student Profile

Prototype a navigable student profile that aggregates academic, formative and contextual data.

Duration: Month 2

Phase 3 — Build & Assessment Diagnostics

Construct initial dashboards and assess existing evaluation systems for alignment.

Duration: Month 3

Phase 4 — AI Readiness & Refinement

Diagnose how AI can support teachers and prepare a roadmap for pilot implementations.

Duration: Month 4

Phase 5 — Consolidation & Roadmap

Deliver final documentation and the operational roadmap to scale the Innovation Area.

Duration: Month 5

Requirements for Success

  • Executive Sponsorship: Active support from Directors and Coordinators.
  • Platform Access: Schooltrack license and Workspace access.
  • Focal Points: Key contacts in critical areas.
  • Agile Governance: Bi-weekly follow-ups.
  • Privacy & Security: Responsible and confidential handling of data.

Next Steps

Validate this plan with the Academic Sub-Directorate, sign the agreement and start Phase 1 focused on delivering the strategic proposal in October.
